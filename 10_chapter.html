9. Use Cases and Applications

The practical applications of AG-UI and A2UI protocols span numerous industries, demonstrating the versatility and transformative potential of agent-driven interfaces.

Official A2UI Demos

The A2UI website at https://a2ui.org/ showcases compelling demonstrations that illustrate the protocol's capabilities:

  • Landscape Architect Demo: An agent that analyzes uploaded photos of outdoor spaces and generates custom landscaping forms. The agent uses computer vision to identify current landscape features, suggests improvements based on space and lighting conditions, and generates A2UI forms for capturing user preferences like budget, style, and plant types
  • Interactive Charts & Maps: Demonstrates how agents choose the right visualization format contextually—geographic data triggers map components, time series data generates line charts, categorical comparisons produce bar charts with interactive filtering
  • A2UI Widget Builder: Available at https://a2ui-composer.ag-ui.com/gallery, this CopilotKit-powered tool lets developers experiment with component generation, preview rendering across form factors, and export configurations
